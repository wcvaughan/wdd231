Mat-Su Trails Explorer

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Mat-Su Trails Explorer website is to provide reliable information about local hiking and recreation trails in the Mat-Su area. This site will help users:
  • Find trails based on difficulty and length
  • View trail descriptions and photos
  • Check weather considerations and seasonal access
  • Learn trail safety tips and responsible outdoor practices

Target Market

Residents of the Mat-Su Valley and tourists visiting the area can utilize the information provided to decide on recreation depending on individual interests and capabilities.

Site Goals

  • Attract new businesses and encourage economic development in the community.
  • Improve the visibility and reputation of the chamber as a trusted authority in local business matters.

Scenarios

  • A new resident of Mat-Su Valley wants to find an easy hiking trail suitable for a weekend family outing. Which trail would be best, and what should they expect in terms of terrain and distance?
  • A visitor traveling to the Mat-Su Valley during the summer wants to know which trails are currently accessible and what weather conditions they should prepare for before hiking.
